Your Complete Journey

1

Prepare Your Application

The foundation of a successful funding application is proper preparation. While our process is streamlined, having the right documents and information ready will ensure the fastest possible approval.

📄 Required Documents
  • ✓ Bank Statements: Last 3 months of business banking activity
  • ✓ Government ID: Driver's license or passport for business owner
  • ✓ Business Registration: Articles of incorporation or business license
  • ✓ Tax Returns: Last 2 years of business tax returns (if available)
💡 Pro Tips for Success
  • 💡 Ensure your bank statements show consistent deposit activity
  • 💡 Have your business plan ready to show how you'll use the funds
  • 💡 Check your personal and business credit scores beforehand
  • 💡 Organize documents digitally for faster upload
⏱️ Preparation Time: 15-30 minutes
❓ Common Questions
What if I don't have all the documents? +
You can start your application with basic information and upload documents later. Our team will guide you through any missing items and suggest alternatives when possible.
How recent do bank statements need to be? +
Bank statements should be from the most recent 3 months. We need current information to assess your business's financial health accurately.
💻
2

Submit Your Application

Our streamlined application process takes just minutes to complete. We've designed it to be comprehensive yet simple, gathering all the information our lending partners need to make informed decisions about your funding request.

📝 Application Sections
  • ✓ Personal Information: Your details as the business owner
  • ✓ Business Details: Company information, industry, time in business
  • ✓ Financial Information: Revenue, expenses, banking relationships
  • ✓ Funding Request: Amount needed and intended use of funds
🔒 Security & Privacy
  • 🔒 256-bit SSL encryption protects all your data
  • 🔒 Bank-level security with SOC 2 compliance
  • 🔒 Your information is never sold to third parties
  • 🔒 Application can be saved and completed later
⏱️ Application Time: 10-15 minutes
❓ Common Questions
Can I save my application and finish later? +
Yes! Your application automatically saves as you progress. You can log back in anytime to continue where you left off.
What happens to my information? +
Your information is shared only with pre-qualified lending partners who can potentially fund your request. We never sell your data to third parties.
