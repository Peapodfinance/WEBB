Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
📝 What This Means for You
We collect only the information necessary to provide you with financing services. We never sell your personal data to third parties. You have complete control over your information and can request its deletion at any time.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, apply for funding, or contact our support team. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, social security number
  • Business Information: Business name, tax ID, industry, revenue data
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, credit history, financial statements
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our platform and services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and evaluate your funding applications
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Send you important updates about your account and our services

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Lending Partners: To process your funding applications
  • Service Providers: Third parties who help us operate our business
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Your data is stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

Types of Cookies We Use

Loan Agreement Terms

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
📝 What This Means for You
When you receive funding through our platform, you'll enter into a separate agreement with the lending partner. We ensure all our partners follow fair lending practices and provide transparent terms.

Our Role

Peapod Finance acts as an intermediary between businesses and lending partners. We do not provide loans directly but facilitate the application and matching process.

Lending Partner Standards

All our lending partners must:

  • Provide clear and transparent loan terms
  • Follow fair lending practices
  • Comply with all applicable regulations
  • Offer reasonable repayment terms
  • Provide excellent customer service

Your Loan Agreement

When approved for funding, you will receive a separate loan agreement from the lending partner that includes:

  • Loan amount and interest rate
  • Repayment terms and schedule
  • Fees and charges
  • Default and collection procedures
  • Your rights and obligations

Support and Assistance

Even after funding, our team remains available to assist with questions about your loan and help resolve any issues that may arise.

Questions About Our Legal Policies?

We believe in transparency and are happy to answer any questions about our legal policies. Our legal and compliance team is here to help.
